Welcome to LetsGraphQL!

An opensource community making the Internet GraphQL-able. 😁

About

We are know that data is one of the most valuable commodities in the modern era and getting it to the right people at the right time and in the most appropriate format.

We achieve this through Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) and there are lots of frameworks to choose from. REST, JSON-RPC, GraphQL, RabbitMQ, etc... However, it is nigh on impossible for businesses to provide each and every interface to support developers in utilising their data.

Here at LetsGraphQL, we ❤ GraphQL as it enables developers to speak in the language of the data they want irrespective of the backend systems that store and power it. Our mission is to make the Internet GraphQL-able. Be it through middleware on existing APIs or working directly with backend systems.

Our opensource projects are funded by our kind and generous sponsors under MIT licenses. Even our main website is opensource!

By providing GraphQL APIs to the Internets data, we aim to maximise the use of data so we can continue to see incredible applications and products that will help society.
